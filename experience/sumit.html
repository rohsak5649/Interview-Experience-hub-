Interview Experience @ DataEconomy

DataEconomy - AI/DS Role

Round 1: Screening Interview
Conducted on campus. Basic technical and HR questions. Cleared and shortlisted for next rounds.

Round 2: Communication Round
Included grammar, comprehension, error spotting, sentence correction, and a 1-minute speech.
Difficulty: Easy

Round 3: Aptitude Round
Covered percentages, ratios, profit & loss, and game-based logic.
Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

Round 4: Technical Assessment
Based on SQL and DSA using Python.
Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

Round 5: Technical Interview
Questions on:

  • Python Cluster
  • DSA, OOPs, SQL
  • Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Projects and Resume
Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

Round 6: HR Interview
Covered:

  • Self-introduction
  • Family background
  • Situational scenarios

Result: Not Selected — but a great learning experience in technical and communication skills.
