Microland - Interview Experience

Round 1 - Online Aptitude Test (30 mins):
Consisted of 30 questions. Covered Verbal Ability, Logical Reasoning, and Quantitative Aptitude. Time management was crucial due to limited time for all three sections.

Round 2 - Core Language Test (40 mins):
Focused mainly on English grammar and language comprehension. Included questions on tenses, prepositions, sentence correction, vocabulary, and reading comprehension.

Round 3 - Speaking/Communication Test (40 mins):
Assessed spoken English fluency, pronunciation, and confidence. Included tasks like reading passages aloud, answering general questions, or speaking on a topic for a minute.

Round 4 - Technical Interview:
Involved core subject knowledge, programming fundamentals, and project discussion. Topics included Java, OOPs, DBMS, or relevant domain knowledge based on the resume.

Round 5 - HR Interview (20 mins):
Focused on personality assessment, communication skills, and cultural fit. Questions covered strengths, weaknesses, career goals, and relocation or salary expectations.
