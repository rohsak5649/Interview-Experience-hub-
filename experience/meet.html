Interview Experience @ Cognizant

Cognizant - Interview Process

Round 1 - Communication Assessment:

  • 30-40 questions across 7 sections
  • Topics included basic grammar, paragraph-based questions, spotting errors, and correcting sentences
  • 1-minute spoken opinion on a given topic

Round 2 - Aptitude Test:

  • Questions on percentage, ratio & proportion, profit & loss
  • Game-based aptitude and analytical reasoning
  • Note: Strong aptitude skills are important to clear this round

Selection Criteria:
Shortlisting for the next round was based on combined results of Round 1 and 2.

  • SQL: 2 questions focused on Joins and related concepts
  • Java: 2 questions including one on Collections

  • Technical Questions: OOPs with real-life examples, DBMS concepts, SQL vs NoSQL, Java coding (patterns, toggle string, bubble sort, selection sort)
