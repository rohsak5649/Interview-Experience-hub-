Interview Experience @ HSBC

HSBC - Interview Process

Timeline:
The process is expected around August–September. All rounds were conducted on-campus, and updates were shared through the official WhatsApp group.

Round 1: Aptitude & Technical MCQ Test
The first round was an online test consisting of aptitude questions along with technical MCQs from core CS subjects.

Round 2: DSA Coding Round
This round had 2 DSA-based coding questions. Questions were of medium difficulty and tested problem-solving ability.

Round 3: Technical Interview
Focus areas included core CS fundamentals like DBMS, OOPs, and understanding every line of the code from your academic/project work. Be ready to discuss your resume and explain the logic behind your project.

Round 4: HR Interview
General behavioral and situational questions were asked, along with discussions on your strengths, career goals, and background.

Preparation Tips:

  • Focus on CS fundamentals: DBMS and OOPs are very important.
  • Practice basic DSA problems (arrays, strings, sorting, etc.).
  • Know your project thoroughly—every single line of it.
  • Stay updated with announcements via the WhatsApp group.

Result: Selected in HSBC
