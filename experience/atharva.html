Interview Experience @ Merkle

Merkle - Analyst Role

Round 1: Aptitude Test
Covered quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, and verbal reasoning. Time management was crucial.

Round 2: Group Discussion
Two topics were given (30 mins each):

  • Learning or Training: Which is more impactful and why?
  • Will AI replace humans?

Round 3: Technical Interview
Discussed preferred role (Data Scientist or Data Engineer) and programming language (Java or Python). Resume-based questions including project work, certifications, and technical skills.

Round 4: Managerial Interview 1
Focused on resume and projects. One coding question: Find common elements from two array lists using preferred language. Also discussed tools used in data analytics.

Round 5: Managerial Interview 2
SQL-based questions, including writing a query to find the 3rd highest salary from a table. Basic concepts of SQL and data science were also tested.

Round 6: HR Interview
Covered personal introduction, office location preference, salary/package discussion, family background, hobbies, and overall feedback on the interview process.

Result: Selected as Analyst at Merkle, Bengaluru.
