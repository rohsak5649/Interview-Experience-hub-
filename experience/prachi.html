Interview Experience @ L&T

L&T - Electronics & Telecommunication Engineer

Overview:
The selection process started with an Online Test Round, followed by a Technical Interview and finally an HR Interview.

1. Online Test Round:

  • Branch-related questions focused on core subjects from Electronics and Telecommunication such as Digital Electronics, Microcontrollers, and Communication Systems.
  • Coding questions tested logical thinking and programming skills in languages like C++ and Python.
  • Problems included pattern printing, basic data structure operations, and logic-building exercises.

2. Technical Interview:

  • Questions on core technical subjects like Digital Electronics and Microprocessor 8085.
  • Basic programming and logic-building tasks were also asked.
  • Final year project discussion: “IoT-Based Mining Tracking and Worker Safety Helmet”
    • Explained usage of components such as ATmega328P, ESP8266 Wi-Fi module, GPS, pulse sensor, and gas sensor.
    • Described the implementation, challenges, and functionality in detail.
  • Scenario-based questions to assess real-world problem-solving and analytical thinking.
  • The interviewer was supportive and encouraged clear communication of ideas.

3. HR Interview:

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Your strengths and weaknesses
  • Are you open to relocation and working in a team?
  • The HR was friendly and the conversation felt natural and relaxed.

Final Thoughts:
The entire interview experience was enriching and helped me grow both technically and personally. I appreciated how the process tested not just theoretical knowledge, but also practical thinking and communication skills.

Result: Selected at L&T

This experience was shared by Prachi Gujrathi, a friend of Rohan Sakhare.
