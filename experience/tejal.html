Interview Experience @ LTI Mindtree

LTI Mindtree - Software Role

Round 1: Online Assessment
This assessment consisted of four sections:

  • Logical Ability: 20 minutes
  • Quantitative Ability: 20 minutes
  • Technical & Computer Programming: 35 minutes
  • Communication Skills: 20 minutes
Level: Easy to Moderate

Round 2: Technical Interview
Focused primarily on DBMS concepts and projects.

  • DBMS questions on Joins, Types of Keys
  • One SQL query writing task
  • Project-based cross-questions: Why specific functions/techniques were used?
Level: Moderate (Good core knowledge is enough)

Round 3: HR Interview
Mainly focused on communication and personality-based questions:

  • Brief self-introduction
  • Why engineering?
  • Willingness to work in shifts?
  • Willing to relocate?
  • Handling two projects with the same deadline?
  • Any active backlogs?
  • Explain your project
Level: Easy to Moderate

Result: Selected at LTI Mindtree
