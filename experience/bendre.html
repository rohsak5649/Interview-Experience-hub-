Interview Experience @ HCLTech

HCLTech - Software Developer

1. Assessment Test:
An online test with aptitude, reasoning, English, and basic programming questions. Some roles include a coding round.

2. Group Discussion:
A group of candidates discusses a topic. It checks your communication, confidence, and ability to work in a team.

3. Technical Interview 1:
Covers programming basics, core subjects like DBMS, OOPs, OS, and your academic projects. You should be able to explain your code clearly.

4. Technical Interview 2:
More detailed technical questions, real-time problem solving, and coding tasks. It tests how you apply your knowledge.

5. HR Interview 1:
Questions about yourself, your goals, your interest in HCLTech, and your flexibility to relocate or work in shifts.

6. HR Interview 2:
A final round if needed. It includes discussions about salary, job role, location, and clearing any remaining doubts.

Result: Selected at HCLTech
