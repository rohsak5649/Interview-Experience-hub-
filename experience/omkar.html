Interview Experience @ HSBC

🏢 Selected for Role at HSBC

📋 Total Rounds: 5

🧠 1. Online Aptitude:
Included standard aptitude questions along with computer fundamentals such as Computer Networks, OOP, and other core CS concepts.

💻 2. Coding Assessment:
Consisted of DSA-based coding questions requiring efficient logic and implementation.

📘 3. HSBC Values Assessment:
This round included scenario-based ethical questions to assess alignment with HSBC’s core values.

🧪 4. Technical Interview (On Campus):
A very creative and detailed round — the interviewer covered a wide range of SDLC domains from basic to advanced concepts in an engaging manner.

🗣️ 5. HR Interview (On Campus):
Covered standard HR questions related to personal background, goals, flexibility, strengths, and workplace readiness.

✅ Verdict: Selected
