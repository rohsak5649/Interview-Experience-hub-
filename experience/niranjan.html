Interview Experience @ LTIMindtree

LTIMindtree - Software Developer Role

Round 1: Online Assessment
This round included quantitative aptitude questions covering:

  • Arithmetic Progression (AP)
  • Geometric Progression (GP)
  • Quadratic Equations
  • Series-based questions
  • Profit & Loss
  • Mixtures
The difficulty level was moderate and manageable within the given time.

Additionally, there was an English communication section. A sentence was displayed on the screen for a few seconds, after which candidates had to speak it aloud clearly and confidently once it disappeared.

Round 2: Technical Interview
Conducted virtually on-campus through individual laptops.
Key areas covered:

  • Self-introduction
  • Resume and project-based questions
  • Certificate discussion
  • OOPs concepts with real-life examples
  • DBMS fundamentals
  • SQL vs NoSQL
  • Java coding: Patterns, Toggle String, Bubble Sort, Selection Sort

Round 3: HR Interview
A casual, personality-focused round. Common questions included:

  • Discussion on hobbies and ambitions
  • Willingness to relocate
  • Availability for rotational shifts

Result: Selected at LTIMindtree
